How to do it?

1. Get ready

Open Patronto and buy a subscription (or claim a trial)

Get your bot token from Botfather and in few seconds your bot will be ready to rock !

2. Setup

Go to the bot you created and create a new package for your service.

Attach channels / groups / information as package benefits.

3. Get paid

Your subscription bot will accept payments and send invite links automatically.

Now your membership business is fully automated and you can focus on content and growth.
